
About

Having trouble looking for the best place to eat? How much will it cost you? Is it good or does the price do you no justice? 
Well look no further because BaguioEats is the perfect app for you. You can easily search for the best restaurants to eat 
at.
BaguioEats is an app that lets you rate food you have eaten at different restaurants in Baguio City.
You can take pictures of the food, put the location of the restaurant and let people know what you think. 
Be honest in giving constructive criticism and help a friend out.
